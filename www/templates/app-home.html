Hi. This application gives a day to day, week to week, and month to month overview of what we are covering in my Algebra 2 class. Hopefully it will give you some idea of where things are going regarding the topics we have been covering. You can also see upcoming scans and quizzes by looking at the weekly agenda. The calendar tab will give you a broad overview of everything we've done, while the weekly agenda is much more specific. The calculator will take a few seconds to load, but once it does you can use it as a great tool to visualize all of the equations and expressions we are covering in class. This application should be available, after an approval process, on Google Play for Android devices and the App Store for iOS. The calculator will render more effectively as an app, it doesn't work very well in a mobile browser. The app will be free, of course. Thanks, and see you in class.
